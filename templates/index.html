About Our Club

TechSpark is the official technical club of Navkis College of Engineering, Hassan. We are a community of passionate students dedicated to learning, building, and innovating. From hackathons to puzzle hunts, we create events that push students to think beyond the classroom. Our motto — Inspire · Innovate · Integrate — drives everything we do.
